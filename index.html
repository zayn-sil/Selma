The Legacy of 4W Circle

In 1991, Selma Jackson, along with three other visionary women, founded 4W Circle of Art and Enterprise in Fort Greene, Brooklyn. For over 17 years, it was a beacon for community and commerce.

More than a boutique, 4W Circle was a pioneering retail incubator that launched the careers of countless African-American women entrepreneurs, providing them with a platform to sell their art, clothing, and accessories.

"We created a space that was unapologetically about us, for us, and by us. It was about economic empowerment and cultural celebration."
